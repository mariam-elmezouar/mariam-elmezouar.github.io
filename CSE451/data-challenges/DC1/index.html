Data Challenge 1

CSE451 Fall 2023

Task: Explore the ratings of FIFA players

Objective: provide a notebook report that explore and visualize the dataset from different perspectives. Document any insights gained from the exploration. Use a mix of statistical techniques and visualizations to achieve this objective.

  • Launch Jupyter Notebook and create a new textbook, and name it DG1_lastname.
  • Download the provided dataset. The dataset contains player ratings for the FIFA World Cup from 2006 to 2018. The columns of the dataset are described below:
    • Player Information:
      • sofifa_id: Unique identifier for each player in the dataset.
      • player_url: URL linking to the player's profile.
      • short_name: The player's abbreviated or short name.
      • long_name: The player's full name.
      • player_positions: The positions the player can play.
      • age: The player's age.
      • dob: Date of birth of the player.
      • height_cm: Height of the player in centimeters.
      • weight_kg: Weight of the player in kilograms.
      • nationality_id: Unique identifier for the player's nationality.
      • nationality_name: The player's nationality.
      • nation_team_id: Unique identifier for the player's national team.
      • nation_position: The player's position in the national team.
      • nation_jersey_number: Jersey number of the player in the national team.
      • preferred_foot: The player's preferred foot for kicking (left or right).
      • weak_foot: The player's weak foot rating.
      • skill_moves: The player's skill moves rating.
      • international_reputation: The player's international reputation.
      • work_rate: The player's work rate.
      • body_type: The player's body type.
      • real_face: Indicates whether the player has a real face (yes or no).
      • player_tags: Tags associated with the player.
      • player_traits: Traits associated with the player.
    • Club Information:
      • club_team_id: Unique identifier for the player's club team.
      • club_name: Name of the player's club team.
      • league_name: Name of the league the player's club team competes in.
      • league_level: The level or tier of the league.
      • club_position: The player's position in the club team.
      • club_jersey_number: Jersey number of the player in the club team.
      • club_loaned_from: If the player is on loan, the name of the club they are loaned from.
      • club_joined: Date when the player joined the club.
      • club_contract_valid_until: Date until which the player's contract with the club is valid.
      • release_clause_eur: Release clause amount in euros.
      • club_logo_url: URL to the club's logo.
      • club_flag_url: URL to the club's flag.
    • Player Attributes: Various columns for player attributes such as pace, shooting, passing, dribbling, defending, and physic, which represent different aspects of a player's skills and abilities in the game. The score is out of 100.
      • pace
      • shooting
      • passing
      • dribbling
      • defending
      • physic
    • Position Ratings: Various columns for specific positional ratings (score out of 100) like attacking_crossing, defending_marking_awareness, goalkeeping_diving, etc., which provide more detailed information about a player's performance in specific aspects of their position.
      • attacking_crossing
      • defending_marking_awareness
      • goalkeeping_diving
      • and more...
    • Player Positions: Columns containing scores (out of 100) for different player positions, including striker (ST), left-winger (LW), right-winger (RW), central attacking midfield (CAM), central midfield (CM), left-back (LB), center-back (CB), right-back (RB), and goalkeeper (GK).
      • ls
      • st
      • rs
      • lw
      • lf
      • cf
      • rf
      • rw
      • lam
      • cam
      • ram
      • lm
      • lcm
      • cm
      • rcm
      • rm
      • lwb
      • ldm
      • cdm
      • rdm
      • rwb
      • lb
      • lcb
      • cb
      • rcb
      • rb
      • gk
    • URLs: URLs for player face image, club logo, club flag, nation logo, and nation flag.
      • player_face_url
      • club_logo_url
      • club_flag_url
      • nation_logo_url
      • nation_flag_url
  • In addition to providing some general visualizations of the data (e.g. histograms + barplots of different columns), attempt to answer some of these questions (tackle as much as you can within the allocated time):
    • General Exploratory Questions:
      1. What is the distribution of player ages in the dataset?
      2. How are player heights distributed across different positions?
      3. What is the average overall rating of players in the dataset?
      4. Which nationality is the most represented among the players?
      5. What is the average wage of players in different age groups?
      6. Is there a correlation between a player's overall rating and their market value?
    • Comparative Exploratory Questions (Comparing Important Players):
      1. Who are the top 5 players with the highest overall ratings in the dataset?
      2. How does the overall rating of Messi compare to Ronaldo in the dataset?
      3. What are the skill attributes that distinguish Cristiano Ronaldo from Lionel Messi in the dataset?
      4. Compare the preferred foot (left or right) of top goal scorers in the dataset.
      5. Compare the international reputation of players from different clubs or leagues.
  • Once happy with your data exploration, clean up the notebook and add explanation/comments/insights as appropriate. Then, submit your notebook on Moodle.
